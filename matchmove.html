KNOW ABOUT MATCH MOVE

Match moving services involve setting coordination between movements and camera angles to develop a perfect shot. Match Move tracking is not a one-hit process it takes hours of efforts of our team members to create that one flawless and absolute scene.

Frame by Frame analysis of the video is done in which the pixels and the subpixels coordinates are matched that falls under the category of motion tracking.

2-D tracking – it is a little less complex than other techniques of the Match Moving services. In 2-D tracking, the primary concern is to just deal with the 2 axis coordinates and set them coordinately to define the scene. This type of match move tracking skill is mainly used to design scenes in which the screen of the I-pad or television is to be displayed.

2-D tracking is way less complex then 3-D tracking techniques but our expert company employees are well trained in every skill to make your video look real and to satisfy you to the fullest.

3-D tracking – the maximum layout of the entire video is been designed by the 3-D tracking techniques. In this match move tracking type all the three-axis – x-axis, y-axis and the depth that is the z-axis is all are considered and carefully timed together so that there are no jerks and unsynchronized movements in the video. Mostly moving crowds or to depict the movements of people or vehicle shots are been directed using the 3-D tracking technique.
